Cacio e Pepe

Description

Cacio e Pepe is an irresistible Roman classic, combining perfectly al dente pasta with a creamy, salty Pecorino Romano sauce and the bold kick of freshly ground black pepper.

This simple yet luxurious dish delivers rich, cheesy warmth and a peppery bite that will leave you craving more.

Ingredients : 4 Servings

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g Pecorino Romano cheese, finely grated
  • 2 tablespoons freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt (for pasta water)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Save some pasta water.
  2. Heat black pepper in a pan, then add a little pasta water.
  3. Mix Pecorino with hot pasta water to form a creamy sauce.
  4. Toss the pasta in the pan with pepper and cheese sauce.
  5. Plate, top with extra Pecorino and pepper, and enjoy!
