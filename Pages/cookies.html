Best Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are chewy, buttery, and filled with melty chocolate chunks, perfect for a sweet treat anytime.

Ingredients : 20 Cookies

  • 225g butter, softened
  • 200g brown sugar
  • 100g granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 300g flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 200g chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Cream butter and sugars.
  2. Mix in eggs and vanilla.
  3. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Bake at 180Â°C for 10-12 minutes.
